WHO IS HE?




Petter is a Norwegian award-winning writer, creative, producer, director, pedant and aspiring douchebag.

This website is a futile attempt at coding for an assignment bestowed upon me by Oslo Met.

I've named this website "A Serious of (U)nfortunate Events" because my life (or everyone's lives for that matter) is a series of unfortunate and/or fortunate events depending on how you look at it.

On this website I will hopefully have demonstrated the following:

  • Rudimentary coding skills.
  • A lack of self-awareness.
  • Narcisistic personality disorder.
  • The ability to induce light chuckling to whomever might be reading this.
